Hello dear listener,

'Should everyone get into tech?' 

This question gets more and more popular every day and for good reason too. Talks about getting into tech increase every day and it may be tempting to think you need to be a programming genius to have a place in the techosystem and that is why in this brand new episode, we invited Luke Ndatigh to talk about the not so popular, not so technical areas of the techosystem.

Luke Ndatigh is an operations specialist at Adyen and in this episode, he discusses his experiences coming from a highly technical background to moving to a less technical role. He also talks about various non-technical roles in tech, how to position yourself to get into some of these roles and answers the big question 'Should everyone get into tech?'
